Improving my Personal Life Balance

This goal was rather simple, achieving a healthy balance in my life in all aspects. Of course, as a dedicated University student, I understand the difficultly of having balance in so many different facets of life. However, I also saw this co-op term as an opportunity to grow.

Since the start of the co-op term I gained a lot of great new relationships with my coworkers. In addition, since I got a car shortly before the start of my co-op term I was able to also travel to a lot of more social events in close cities. Although I still have a long way to go, I believe I have improved my life balance as I was able to succeed in the workplace and maintain healthy social relationships.
