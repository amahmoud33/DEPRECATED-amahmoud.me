At the end of my S18/F18 co-op term I happily accepted the opportunity to return to Unitron after a great start to my software development career. After a successful first term I was very comfortable as a software developer at Unitron and know that there is where I wanted to be. During both terms I got opportunities to reflect on meeting Unitron customers who had their life changed by our products which is fulfilling. In addition, the mentorship provided by the other developers helped me grow both as a developer and as a person.

In addition, I met many fellow Guelph Gryphons who were on the Unitron team as co-ops. I reflect on the fun co-op events, BBQs, and conversations over the term. In fact, we had a record number of co-ops this term and 4 of them were Guelph Gryphons!
