At the start of 2018 I was given an amazing opportunity to begin my career at an amazing company. Going into the tech showcase I had heard about Unitron and when meeting their employees, I knew that’s where I wanted to be. At the end of the term I reflect on the fun employee events, meeting Unitron customers who had their life changed by our products and growing under an experienced and supportive development team.

In addition, as a Unitron employee I had numerous meetings where I learned about the different types of hearing loss and how it affects people. We even had another Gryphon, Dar’ya Heyko, who gave us an amazing perspective into life with hearing loss and how hearing aid products changed her life. Seeing this first hand gives purpose to our work and is a reward. Hopefully this report reflected my wonderful experience as a software developer at Unitron.
